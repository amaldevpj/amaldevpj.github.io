Live · GenAI

IntelliRAG — Adaptive Knowledge Assistant

A production-style Retrieval-Augmented Generation system that answers questions from your own documents with cited, source-grounded responses. Built on FastAPI: it ingests and semantically chunks documents, runs hybrid retrieval (semantic plus BM25 with reciprocal rank fusion) and cross-encoder reranking, then generates answers with citations and confidence scoring. Containerised with Docker and built with monitoring and CI/CD in mind.
